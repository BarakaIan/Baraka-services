Welcome

Welcome to Iana Designs, the home of beautiful, unique, spectacular designs in 254.

Iana Designs was created in 2018 in an effort to fill the niche in the fashion industry, be it clothing, shoes or bags, laptop covers or jewelery.

Our Mission

Driven by the need to transform the fashion industry, we decided to open this great fashion line in an effort to improve the look of our customers, by ensuring that we provide them with unique, smart and affordable products.

Our Products

  • Handbags
  • Jewelery
  • Snickers
  • Necklaces

Future Goals

We are not only a profit oriented company, but also look into giving back to the community by supporting the needy students through scholarships and skills.

By the year 2021, we hope to increase our sales by reaching untapped markets across the globe with our products.

We also plan to open more stores in other countries to reach more of our clients
